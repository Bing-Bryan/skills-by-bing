# Personal Xianyu copy standard

## Structure: 人—货—况—证—价—交

Treat the six sections as a menu, not a fixed template: include a section only when it has real content for this item, and merge or drop the rest. An item with no accessories omits 【随套配件】; unknown purchase facts omit 【购入情况】. Use visible anchors because Xianyu may compress line breaks.

1. `【先说重点】`: personal ownership, purchase time, reason for sale, bundle preference, price if useful.
2. Use a category-appropriate core-item heading such as `【商品与配件】`: list the item and decisive bundle first; explain only buyer-relevant use.
3. `【真实成色】`: function, material history, category-relevant condition, and visible flaws with photo references.
4. `【随套配件】`: only when accessories exist; group them with semicolons and do not let gifts dominate the listing.
5. `【购入情况】`: original price and expired warranty only when confirmed and useful.
6. `【交易说明】`: bundle/split preference, shipping, inspection, extra photos, and personal no-warranty language.

## Mobile readability

- Keep each section to 1–4 short lines.
- Use `【】`, semicolons, and circled numerals or short bullets.
- Avoid a continuous numbered inventory of more than five lines.
- Put the strongest decision facts in the first screen: model, bundle, condition, price, and sale reason.
- Confirm the rendered page after publishing; source newlines are not enough.

## Voice

Write like the actual owner: natural, specific, calm, and slightly conversational.

Prefer: `个人自用`, `现在用得少`, `整套优先`, `细节见最后三张`, `需要哪个角度可以补拍`.

Avoid merchant language: `性价比天花板`, `闭眼入`, `到手即用`, `全网最低`, `整套省心`, excessive feature marketing, and inflated gift values.

Avoid hostile language: `大刀拉黑`, `事多勿扰`, `介意勿拍`. State boundaries neutrally.

## Title

Use this order:

`ownership cue + brand/model + decisive bundle + one trust/detail cue`

Example: `个人自用 + brand/model + decisive bundle + one trust cue`

Do not repeat the full title as the first body paragraph unless the page has no separate title field and uses the first line as the title.

## Review checklist

- Does the first screen answer what, condition, bundle, and why selling?
- Are every defect and uncertain fact explicit?
- When accessories exist, are they subordinate to the core item?
- Does each section remain readable if newlines collapse?
- Is the copy personal rather than promotional?
- Are transaction terms compatible with platform rules?

## Canonical copy and Xianyu rendering

Keep a single canonical plain-text version for both confirmation and publication. The live detail page—not the editor—is the source of truth for layout.

For PC contenteditable publishing, ordinary Enter-separated lines may look correct in the editor while being concatenated after save. Encode logical breaks as persisted `<br>` elements, then verify the live description line by line. Preserve:

- one line per section heading;
- one line per numbered accessory or component;
- one blank visual line between sections;
- identical wording and order between the confirmed copy and the live item.
